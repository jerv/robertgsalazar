Interventional Treatment

The following procedures are performed at the Comprehensive Pain management Center by Dr. Salazar. Dr. Salazar does not perform "in office" treatment.

No injections of analgesics are available in the office.

Invasive modalities (injections)
  • Atlanto-axial
  • Atlanto-occipital
  • Caudal epidural steroid
  • Cervical discography
  • Cervical medial branch
  • Cervical radiofrequency lesion
  • Cervical therapeutic facets
  • Cervical transforaminal epidural steroid
  • Cervical translaminar epidural steroid
  • First dorsal compartment
  • Genitofemoral
  • Greater occipital nerve
  • Greater trochanteric bursa
  • Ilioinguinal nerve
  • Implantation and revisions of intrathecal drug delivery systems
  • Implantation and revisions of spinal cord stimulation systems
  • Intercostal nerve
  • Intra-articular hip and shoulder
  • Intrathecal trial, baclofen and opioid
  • iOvera knee treatment
  • Lateral femoral cutaneous nerve
  • Levator scapular nerve
  • Lumbar discography
  • Lumbar medial branch
  • Lumbar radiofrequency lesion
  • Lumbar sympathetic ganglion
  • Lumbar therapeutic facet
  • Lumbar transforaminal epidural steroid
  • Lumbar translaminar epidural steroid
  • Median nerve
  • Peripheral nerves
  • Piriformis muscle
  • Rhomboid muscles
  • Sacroiliac joint
  • Sacroiliac radiofrequency lesion
  • Stellate ganglion
  • Suprascapular nerve
  • Symphysis pubis
  • Thoracic therapeutic facet
  • Thoracic translaminar epidural steroid
  • Transsacral
Procedures we do not perform:
  • IDET (intradiscal electrothermal annuloplasty)
  • MILD (minimally invasive lumbar decompression)
  • Vertebral augmentation
  • Vertebroplasty
  • Kyphoplasty
  • Celiac plexus neurolysis
