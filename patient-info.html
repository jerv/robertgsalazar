Becoming a Patient

Your initial evaluation will include a thorough history and physical examination.

Performing a complete and through evaluation of your pain condition is our primary concern therefore we require you to be referred by your medical provider who is familiar with your pain condition. This facilitates obtaining results and documentation of previous medical information pertaining to your condition prior and the opportunity for Dr. Salazar to review the results of submitted records prior to scheduling your appointment.

Once your referral is reviewed and appointment is scheduled we will mail the appropriate forms for completion. Your initial evaluation will include a thorough examination and history taking at which time Dr. Salazar may order additional studies to identify the cause of your pain and recommend a treatment plan

Dr. Salazar may recommend additional diagnostic testing. A decision will be made regarding long-term pharmalogical management.
